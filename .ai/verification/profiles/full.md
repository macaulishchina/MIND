# Verification Profile: Full

Use this profile for high-risk changes, broad changes, or final pre-archive
validation of substantial work.

## Required Checks

- `spec-consistency`
- `workflow-integrity`
- `change-completeness`
- `behavior-parity`
- `manual-review`

## Typical Use

- broad multi-file changes
- cross-capability changes
- any change being archived after meaningful implementation work

## Success Standard

There is strong evidence that the change matches its approved intent, its
workflow artifacts are complete, and known residual risk is recorded.
