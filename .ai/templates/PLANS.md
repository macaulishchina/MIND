# Execution Plan

> Copy this file to `PLANS.md` at the repo root for any large or multi-step
> change. Keep it updated while the work is in progress.

## Goal

- What outcome are we trying to achieve?

## Why Now

- Why is this change needed now?
- What user need, defect, or debt does it address?

## Constraints

- What must not change?
- What interfaces, performance, compatibility, or rollout limits apply?

## Non-Goals

- What is explicitly out of scope for this plan?

## Affected Areas

- Which modules, tests, docs, and rules are likely to change?

## Risks

- What could break?
- What is hard to verify?

## Steps

1. Define the first small, testable slice.
2. List each planned step in execution order.
3. Note expected artifacts or verification after each step.

## Verification

- Which commands, tests, gates, or manual checks will confirm correctness?

## Progress Log

- `todo` — pending work item
- `doing` — current step
- `done` — completed step with short note

## Decisions

- Record important decisions and why they were made.

## Open Questions

- Capture unresolved questions here instead of hiding them in chat history.
